Introduction

A short poem

He works each day to be his best, To rise above and pass the test. He's focused on self-improvement's call, And strives to be his very all.


She sees the work he's putting in, And loves the growth that she has seen. She cherishes each small advance, And cheers him on in each new stance.


She knows the path to self-growth's long. And so she helps him to stay strong. She appreciates all that he does, And supports him in his worthy cause.


Together, they make a team, A force to be reckoned and seen. He's improving, and she's right there, And with each step, they zare.
